# Crepes (dessert)

## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup all purpose flour 
- 1 egg
- 0.25 cup water
- 0.25 cup milk
- 1/8 tsp salt 
- 1 tbsp butter, liquid

## Instructions

- Whisk flour and egg together. Gradually add milk and water. Add salt and butter 
- Cook 1 minute on each side in pan
- Fill with fillings and dust with sugar
