# Hummus (dip)

## Ingredients
- 2 can **chickpeas** (juice of 1 can, drain other) or 1 can chickpeas and 0.25 cup juice
- 5 tbsp / 75 ml **lemon juice**
- 0.25 cup **olive oil**
- 2 tbsp / 30 ml **tahini**
- **garlic** 

## Tools
- blender

## Instructions

1. Blend ingredients. Add layer of additional olive oil and additional paprika on top.
