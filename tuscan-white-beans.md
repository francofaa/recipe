% Tuscan White Beans

## Ingredients

- 2 garlic cloves
- 1/4 cup red pepper flakes
- 1 chopped tomato or 1 can tomatoes
- 1 cup parmasean
- 2 cans cannellini beans
- 1 onion
- Rosemary
- parsley

## Instructions

- Saute onion, garlic, red pepper flakes, tomato.

- Rinse beans. Add into mixture. Mash beans up. Add parmesan.
